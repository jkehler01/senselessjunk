My Photo Collections

Here are a few collections of photos I have taken. I have been into photography since I was about 10. I got my first real 35mm camera about that time. It was a Yashica model something or other. I remember that it wasn't an SLR but I didn't know any better. It did take great photos and I learned alot. The next camera I got was a Pentax K-1000. I didn't know when I bought it, but that camera was used heavily in schools and instructional settings. I loved that camera and used it to take some amazing photos. I guess I had that camera for about 10 years.

Then in 1998 or so I became exposed to digital cameras. I was lucky to have free access to many models and played with them a lot but the quality was just not there at that time. I resisted giving up my film and my Nikon N-80!

Once technology and cost were in line I finally accepted digital when I played with the Nikon D series. I had the N-80 and lots of lenses and accessories so the obvious choice was to buy a D-80 body and give it a shot. Let me simply say, I love it. The freedom and flexibility of digital is awesome and now that the quality is there, with few execptions, film is a memory.

Below you can see some of the recent results from the D-80!

Lightning

After years of attempting to capture the "magic" of lightning strikes, I finally did it.

In April 2009, we had a nasty thunderstorm approaching just after dusk. It provided ideal conditions for some great shots. So I grabbed the Nkon and hit the front porch. I took about 200 photos to get these few "money shots" but it was well worth it to me.
